# Clinical Validation Checklist

- Check whether important model features align with known stroke risk factors
- Verify whether age, hypertension, heart disease, glucose, BMI and smoking are influential
- Interpret work type, residence type and marital status cautiously
- Avoid overstating correlation as medical causation
- Under class imbalance, focus on recall, F1-score and ROC-AUC, not only accuracy
- Compare model outputs with literature-supported stroke risk patterns
